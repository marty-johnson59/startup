Biking Pics

Great variety of trails and road rides - with unparalleled views

Phils Trails - excellent network of trails just outside Bend (10 min from my house..)

Lava Butte/Benham Falls trails - Great trails along the Deschutes and the lava flow. Other-worldly...

Maston trails - really nice set of trails down in Tumalo - along the Deschutes. Nice river and Mt. views

And of course, there's stellar road riding around here too...

Pro racing comes through Bend every year too. You might recognize Chris Horner in the green - he was the new announcer for Tour De France last year - a local to Bend.

Thanks to Pete and Ben for contributing some road pics. Also to Tim, Lisa and Lynd for some mt. biking pics.
